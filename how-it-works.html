how it works

On Arrival:

Tags, markers, scotch tape and Measuring Tape will be given along with baskets.

reserve your items:

To reserve large items use your tags. Write your name on the tag and tape it to the item you want. Smaller items can be carried in the basket therefore no tag will be needed.

Change your mind?

Remove the tag from the item and keep looking for the objects that speak to you.

Ready for Checkout?

Make your way to the checkout counter to complete your purchases. A staff member will help gather your reserved items, we will process cash only payment and load your vehicle.

require delivery?

We have a third party delivery professional onsite to quote delivery for larger purchases.

do you offer returns or exchanges?

We do not offer returns or exchanges at this time. All sales are final. Our objects have lived and therefore will have age and wear. Our items are sold as is so please inspect before leaving Chattel.
