About Us

We create new home comforts with home furnishings and housewares for clutter-freeness, organisation and summer vacation-like enjoyment.

how it works

what is a drop?

We drop unique objects and pieces on a first come, first serve basis. We are open to the public only when drops occur. The date, time and location of the drops are annouced to our community via Email, Instagram, Whatsapp and our website. Drops happen only in person for now.

where is your location?

We are located in the island of Barbados in Cane Garden, St.Andrew.

do you offer returns or exchanges?

We do not offer returns or exchanges at this time. All sales are final. Our objects have lived and therefore will have age and wear. Our items are sold as is so please inspect before leaving Chattel.
